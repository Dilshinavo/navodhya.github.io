Build Listening Skills





Childhood is a time filled with excitement and wonder. It is also a period in which kids learn a great deal, in order to develop physically, cognitively, socially and emotionally. Strong listening skills are connected to all four of these areas. You can practise listening skills in early childhood through play, in order to give your kids a solid foundation.

What Are Good Listening Skills And Why Are They Important?

When you think about listening skills, the physical health of the ears sometimes comes to mind first.  Beyond the ability to hear well, however, listening is connected to what our brain understands and how it uses the sounds we take in and process. Importance Of Build Listening Skills Trough Playing

  • Build relationships - Personal, Social & Emotional Development.
  • Creativity - Expressive Arts & Design.
  • Develop language - Communication & Language.
  • Build strength - Physical Development.
  • Build blocks for specific areas of learning - Literacy, Maths & Understanding the World.
  • Problem-solving skills - Maths.
