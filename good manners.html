TUTOR FOR GOOD MANNERS


Manners means polite behavior that shows other people respect, courtesy, and kindness. Manners should be a fundamental step in every child's learning. It's never too early to start learning manners, Teaching manners to children of early childhood is powerful for parents and children alike! It helps children navigate their own social world and grow into healthy, happy adolescents and adults.


Why Is Teaching Good Manners Important To Your Child?

  • Good manners lead to stronger and more positive relationships.
  • Boosts self-esteem.
  • Manners refer to polite and good social behaviour.
  • Good manners result in happier children.
  • They show others that you care about them.

Good Manners Every Child Should Learn

  • Being kind and helping those in need.
  • Speaking the truth.
  • Listening with patience.
  • Responding politely and promptly.
  • Apologizing for mistakes.
  • Taking care of the elderly.
  • Refraining from using foul words.
  • Being grateful for someone's help.

Ways To Teach Your Child Good Manners

  • Set an example by greeting others with kindness and respect.
  • Introduce stories, shows, or rhymes with moral values.
  • Address their negative behaviors and educate the right ways.
  • Acknowledge their good behavior as a motivation.
  • Talk to you kids about why they need to have nice manners.
